Introducing WAPL ID

Your Career, Verified and Visible.

WAPL ID is a career readiness identity designed to help students understand their skill level, build a verified professional profile, and gain meaningful visibility with recruiters. Through structured skill readiness assessment and interview-focused support, WAPL ID guides students toward real opportunities by preparing them for interviews rather than making unrealistic job promises.
